Hi! I am Alberto Gragera, an engineering manager based in Zurich currently working at Google.

Before Google I worked at WhatsApp, [Karumi](https://karumi.com) (a small consultancy company that I co-founded) and [Tuenti](https://tuenti.com) (the biggest Spanish social network ever). I started coding at 12, building forums and websites for fun, and never stopped.

I care deeply about how teams work — the dynamics, the culture, the systems that make collaboration either soar or sink. Most of what I write here reflects that.

You can find me on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/gragera_) and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gragera).
